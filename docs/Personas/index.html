Who are they?

    The target users of our system are emergency institutions such as police, medical services and firefighters/paramedics. The main actors are:

  • Emergency Institution: Emergency Institution Agents have access to all the information related to the emergency they are supporting.

  • Administration team: The Administration team has access to all data, has permissions to change it, and has access to error logs for possible updates and fixes of the system.

Peter: Policeman

This is Peter, he is 32 years old, and he is a Recent-Graduate Policeman.
“Since i was a kid my dream was to be a policeman, so I could help other people and become a better person. I live in Aveiro and as you can imagine is a city with a lot of traffic and people running arround”
Recently he got assigned to traffic accidents department.
“Im new at this department so I dont have the same level of expertise as my colleagues, and i'm afraid that I can't be able to manage all the accidents at the same time. It would be welpfull if there was a system that give me real time information so that I can assess the situation the best I can. ”

Samuel: Paramedic

This is Samuel, he is 44 years old, and he is a Paramedic.
"I'm living in Aradas, and I have 2 daughters studying Biomedical Engeneering and Informatics both at University of Aveiro. After I've graduated at Mario sacramento school, I was wondering what i could do to help other poeple so, I went to the firefight department of Aveiro and I started working and studying to be a paramedic until now."
When there is an accident paramedics are the ones that go there to rescue people.
"I love the rush and adrenaline that is going on a rescue mission, but until we get all the information of the accident we can't do much. Each accident is different and we try to gather all the necessary equipment to save as many lives as possible but sometimes due to the lack of information we can't do our job properly. If there was some way to see the accident so we could know the number of people involved and understand the severity of the accident, we could do a better management of our Team availability but also of our equipment."

Rose: System Manager

This is Rose, she is 26 years old, and she is a System Manager.
"In highshool I started to get really intererested in Computer Science and IT, so I decided to take on the course of Informatics Engineering at University of Aveiro. After I graduated, i started working in a clothing company as a Web Designer. I worked there for 2 years, in which I was able to gain some soft skills on team and project managment and increase my knowledge of Web Development. When I knew about Project ARC, it immediatly caught my attention, since I thought it could really improve the quality and safety of driving and help the emergency services to act faster, so I applied for the System Manager position."
The System Manager is the main authoroty of the system
"As a system manager, I have the responsabilty of ensuring that the Project ARC Web App runs correctly, without errors, so that the emergency services can access the accidents data as fast as possible and rescue the injured in those accidents. At the same time, I'm working in the professional field that I love, which is gratifying."
